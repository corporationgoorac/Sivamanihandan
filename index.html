My Story

Siva Manikandan is an independent music artist from India creating emotionally driven, story-based songs that connect deeply with listeners. His journey into music began when life took an unexpected turn, encouraging him to express honest emotions through sound.

Working independently, he taught himself songwriting and composition, focusing on sincerity rather than trends. His latest release “Po Na Paathaiyil” reflects this growth, marking a new chapter in his artistic journey.
