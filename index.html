Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

At Goorac, we prioritize your privacy through our "Quantum Inbox" philosophy: secure, transparent, and user-centric data handling.

1. Data Collection

We collect minimal information to provide our core messaging services:

  • Account Information: Your display name, chosen username, bio, and profile picture.
  • Authentication: We use Firebase Auth to securely manage your login credentials.
  • Metadata: Timestamps of messages, delivery/seen status, and your online/offline presence.

2. Quantum Inbox Security

  • Real-time Presence: Your "Active Now" status is managed via Firebase Realtime Database and is only visible to those you are communicating with.
  • Message Privacy: Messages are stored in private sub-collections within Firestore. We do not use your message content for advertising purposes.
  • Zero-Latency Cache: For performance, some data is stored locally on your device (LocalStorage). This data remains on your device and is not accessible by our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

Goorac utilizes Google Firebase for backend infrastructure, including Firestore, Authentication, and Realtime Database. By using Goorac, you also agree to the Google Privacy Policy.
